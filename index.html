Welcome to My Economy Bot

This bot is designed to enhance your server's economy with various features. Earn credits by working or begging, and spend them in the shop to buy items. Keep track of your balance and become the richest member in the server!

Commands:

  • !start: Begin your economic journey.
  • !work: Work hard and earn credits.
  • !beg: Beg for credits.
  • !shop: Browse available items in the shop.
  • !buy <item>: Purchase an item from the shop.
